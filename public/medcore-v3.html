Cardiovascular Pharmacology
QUESTION
What is the mechanism of action of beta-blockers?
Click to reveal answer
ANSWER
Beta-blockers competitively antagonize catecholamines at β-adrenergic receptors. β1 blockade → ↓ HR, contractility, BP. β2 blockade → bronchoconstriction. Non-selective: propranolol. Cardioselective: metoprolol, atenolol, bisoprolol.
